Leaving Lagos

Leaving Lagos

Leaving Lagos was definitely an emotional moment as I was leaving the country I had lived in for 19 years of my life, not knowing when I would return. Onboard the flight, I tried to get some sleep but the excitement just wouldn't let me. I spent most of the time looking out the window, staring at the dark, before trying to watch something to keep me busy. Leaving at night made the moment even more surreal.

Arriving in London

We had a 7-hour layover in London, so we spent most of the time at my dad's personal tailor.

Lunch At Heathrow

After we were done at the tailor's, we went back to the airport. We decided to have lunch there because we didn’t want to risk missing our flight by going into the city. I settled for yakisoba, which I didn’t enjoy much, but hey — 10/10 for presentation!

Flight To Montreal

Once we boarded the last flight to Montreal, we were parked at the gate for about 2 hours (yes, 2 hours!) waiting for some paperwork to be completed before takeoff.
